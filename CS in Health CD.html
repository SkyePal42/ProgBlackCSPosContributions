Impact of Computer Science on the Health Industry

Computer Science has had an impact on almost all aspects of the health industry - from diagnostics, research and treatment to operational management. The use of technology in health care continues to grow and innovations continue to be made.

Bioinformatics

Bioinformatics, which uses computational processes to examine large amounts of biological data, has brought a huge amount of progress to healthcare. Bioinformatics reduces both the financial cost of research and the time taken. For example, bioinformatics made forecasting particular molecules which may inhibit COVID-19 possible, so that laboraties knew which to target in their testing. Bioinformatics can narrow down large data sets in to smaller plausible sets and thereby give direction to research.

Bioinformatics can also make a difference on a level closer to individual communities - rudimentary bioinformatics tools are already being used in treating patients.
